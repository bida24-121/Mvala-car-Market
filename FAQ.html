Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of vehicles do you sell? ▸

We sell a wide range of vehicles including new cars, used cars, SUVs, and trucks from trusted brands to suit various needs and budgets.

2. Can I trade in my old car? ▸

Yes! We offer trade-in options. Bring your current vehicle for evaluation and we’ll offer you a fair market value towards your next purchase.

3. Do you offer car financing? ▸

Absolutely. We partner with multiple financial institutions to provide competitive financing options to make car ownership easy and affordable.

4. Are your vehicles inspected? ▸

Yes. Every vehicle on our lot goes through a detailed inspection and quality check to ensure it meets our standards for safety and performance.

5. Do you offer warranties? ▸

Many of our vehicles come with warranties. Extended warranty options are also available for added peace of mind.

6. Can I book a test drive? ▸

Of course! Simply contact us or visit our location to schedule a test drive at your convenience.

7. What documents do I need to purchase a car? ▸

You’ll typically need a valid ID, proof of address, and proof of income. If financing, additional documentation may be required.

8. Do you deliver vehicles? ▸

We offer delivery services for selected locations. Contact us to find out if we can deliver to your area.
